Business challenges

Data importance

Environmental data is an important source of information for social domains such as policy, legislation, taxation and investments. After all, the data is used to identify trends, to uncover connections and to demonstrate patterns.

Data vulnerability

The important role of the data from these types of measurements makes them vulnerable to influence by parties with an interest in the developments within such a social domain.

Digitization

Digitisation is key to drive the sustainability transformation forward. Data insights, combined and mined creatively, help us better understand our true impact, identify the most effective levers for change, and are also key to enabling circular solutions at scale.

Audited IoT devices
