SOLUTION

Decentralized commercial lease agreements

Legal documents used to be static pieces of paper. Through Live Contracts, unique automation and efficiency can be achieved.

Involved parties

CMS Law, MSeven Real Estate

Business challenges

Use of paper

Paper contracts have been used for centuries to formalize agreements. By using paper contracts, we use natural language that is describing the procedures that relate to the promises that are made in an agreement. This works, but it can only be used as a static reference.

Legal Automation

TIt’s difficult for computers to understand any natural language. This means doing automation based on paper contracts is almost impossible. The proposed holy grails, Smart contracts, often don’t suffice and are too rigid.

Many stakeholders

On commercial lease agreements, many parties are involved, such as maintenance, tenants, landlords, asset managers, asset owners, etc. These parties all need to be aligned, ideally looking at the same set of real-time data.

Solution overview

One point-of-truth

By utilising blockchain technology, each party will have control over its own data and will be able to verify the actions of others, making cooperation secure. Directly linking IoT-devices to the blockchain eliminates any form of human interference.

The building passport

Especially new buildings can start off the right way by creating a building passport, that allows for a complete, trusted set of data which can be transferred when switching owners.

Organizations’ own

Organizational environmental data is now scattered all over the place. In future white label environments, each organization will have its own dashboard, with its own data, to do their own analysis on that data.

Advantages using LTO Network

Automated compliance

Save up to 40% on administrative overhead by reducing bureaucracy within organization. The LTO platform creates automated audit trails, and orchestrated tasks. Its status can be checked by the actors without relying on a central organization.

Ease of integration

LTO Network is a fully decentralized and highly efficient blockchain infrastructure that is easy to integrate in existing (legacy) systems. Production-ready for instant efficiency upgrades.

Liquidity

Liquidity improvement and less write offs on tenants who go bankrupt because service charges can be debited directly instead months after the close of the year.
