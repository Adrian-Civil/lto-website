About LTO Network

Organizations explore blockchain for the cost-savings and efficiency that can be gained through trustless collaboration. The same way we did in 2017, as a B2B software company ourselves.

As we experienced, smart contracts had severe limitations when it came to daily business operations. That's why we built LTO Network. A platform for optimized timestamping and efficient collaboration using Live Contracts.

Tech: Hybrid Blockchain

LTO Network’s tech makes it the most flexible blockchain for business that can be applied to almost every use case. Permissioned blockchains are passe. Permissionless hybrid blockchains are the future.

Use the public blockchain for the notarization of data, transactions and events in private blockchains. Our public chain stands out for it’s cost-efficiency, speed and ease of integration.

The ad-hoc miniature private blockchains enable B2B-collaboration through structured, secure and efficient data-exchange. These so-called Live Contracts that run between parties define both on-chain logic and off-chain instructions for new- and legacy systems!
